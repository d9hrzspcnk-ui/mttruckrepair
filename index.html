Before You Call

Common Questions

Do you work on trailers and reefers?
Yes — dry vans, reefers, and flatbeds: lights and wiring, air lines and valves, brakes, doors, floors, landing gear, and mud flaps. For the refrigeration unit itself, call us and we will point you in the right direction.
What are your rates?
Every job gets a written estimate up front before work begins — no surprises on the invoice. Fleet accounts get negotiated labor rates and parts pricing.
What payment do you take?
Cash, all major cards, Zelle, Venmo, and CashApp. Approved fleet accounts get Net-30 terms with monthly statements.
How fast can you do a DOT inspection?
Usually same-day if you call ahead. Federal annual and 90-day inspections, with the paperwork for your compliance file.
Can I wait with my truck?
Yes. You can also check in from your phone before you arrive, and we will text you updates while you wait or run errands.
Will you do extra work without asking?
Never. If our technician finds something beyond the original job, you get the findings with photos and a price sent to your phone — nothing happens until you authorize it with your signature.
My truck will not move — do you tow?
We are not a tow company, but our road service can often get you rolling right where you sit — batteries, air leaks, brakes, electrical. If it truly needs a hook, dispatch will help you coordinate one.
Do you offer credit or financing?
Fleet accounts can apply for Net-30 terms. For larger repairs, call the shop and we will work out options before the job starts.
