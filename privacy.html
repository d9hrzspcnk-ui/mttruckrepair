Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

MT Truck and Trailer Repair ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Burguete Enterprise Inc., respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

When you do business with us or contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name and business name
  • Your mobile phone number and email address
  • Your vehicle information (make, model, year, VIN, mileage)
  • Service and repair history, invoices, and estimates

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to operate our repair business and serve you, including to:

  • Perform and document repairs and maintenance on your vehicle
  • Send you invoices, estimates, appointment reminders, and vehicle-ready notifications
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer service
  • Keep records required for our business and tax purposes
SMS / Text Messaging: Mobile phone numbers and SMS consent are collected to send you service-related text messages about your repair. We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or SMS consent with any third parties or affiliates for their marketing purposes. No mobile information is shared with third parties for marketing. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third parties.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information when necessary to operate (for example, a payment processor to handle a payment you authorize, or as required by law). We never share your phone number or messaging consent for third-party marketing.

Text Message Opt-Out

You can stop receiving text messages from us at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or contact us at 909-600-8440. See our Messaging Terms for details.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your data.
